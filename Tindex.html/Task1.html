Maggiee Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Water
  • 1 Packet of Maggiee
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 Onion

Steps:

  1. On the Stove and place the bowl
  2. After heating the bowl and 2 cups of water.
  3. After water gets boiled add maggiee into it.
  4. Then add salt to it .
  5. Cook until it gets fried.
  6. Serve warm with some onions to it.
